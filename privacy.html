# ChibiBot Privacy Policy **Effective Date:** July 20, 2026 Welcome to the ChibiBot Privacy Policy. ChibiBot is a Discord bot developed for the **AORUS India Discord Community**. This Privacy Policy explains what information the bot processes, how it is used, and the choices available to users. --- ## 1. Information We Process To provide its services, ChibiBot may process the following Discord information: - Discord User ID - Discord Server (Guild) ID - Discord Channel ID - Discord Message IDs - Support ticket information - Commands issued to the bot - AI conversation prompts (only when AI features are used) - Basic moderation logs ChibiBot does **not** collect passwords, payment information, or other sensitive personal information. --- ## 2. How We Use Information The information processed by ChibiBot is used only to: - Provide moderation features - Manage support tickets - Respond to user commands - Generate AI-assisted responses - Improve server management - Prevent abuse and spam - Maintain service reliability --- ## 3. AI Features Certain features may use third-party AI providers to generate responses. Only the content necessary to generate a reply is transmitted. These providers process data in accordance with their own privacy policies. --- ## 4. Data Storage Depending on enabled features, ChibiBot may temporarily store: - Ticket history - Moderation logs - Configuration settings - AI conversation history (if enabled) Data is retained only as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service. --- ## 5. Data Sharing ChibiBot does **not** sell, rent, or trade user data. Information is only shared when required to: - Provide AI responses - Comply with applicable laws - Protect the security of the service --- ## 6. User Rights Users may request deletion of stored ticket or AI conversation data where technically feasible. --- ## 7. Security Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect stored information. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security. --- ## 8. Children's Privacy ChibiBot is intended for Discord users who comply with Discord's Terms of Service. --- ## 9. Changes to this Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. The latest version will always be available in this repository. --- ## 10. Contact For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the AORUS India moderation team through the official Discord server. --- Last Updated: July 20, 2026
